Gogte Institute Of technology

Gogte Institute of Technology, the flagship Institute of Karnatak Law Society, Belagavi was incepted in 1979 and it is included under the section 2(f) and 12(b) of UGC act 1956, permanent affiliate and an Autonomous Institution under Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. Spread across a sprawling mango meadow of over 23 Acres, GIT, houses 07 under graduate and 09 post graduate disciplines including MBA and MCA programs. GIT has the honour of sheltering over 4500 students and more than 250 highly qualified and motivated work force. Institute is also carries the distinction of getting NBA Accreditation by AICTE for the third time in succession, 2004, 2008 and 2015. In the recent visit of NBA, five of its programmes viz. Civil Engg., Mechanical Engg., Electrical & Electronics Engg., Electronics & Communication Engg. and Computer Science & Engg. conferred with accreditation status. A degree from the accredited programme has global acceptability and assurance about the quality of education. Institute is accredited with grade A+ by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Research and innovation has been key strength of GIT. A band of research scholars pursue for Ph.D. and M.Sc. (Engineering) by Research through approved research centers in all the streams including Management studies. Excellent infrastructure in areas like Flexible Manufacturing System with CNC Machines and ROBOTICS, CAD/CAM, Digital Signal Processing, High Voltage Engineering, VLSI etc. catered by a full-fledged 10/100 Mbps internet with OFC backbone and 150 Mbps Internet Connectivity. 1300+ Computers in the Campus make GIT as one of the best destinations for Technology. The institute has received funds of more than a one crore from various agencies such as AICTE, UGC, VTU, VGST for modernization of the laboratory facilities and for research work.
